describing the recipe

Deliciously lemony without being overly tart, these decadent French toast slices are sweet, tasty, and easy to whip up for a last minute breakfast.

  • 5 large egg whites
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 6 slices bread

steps

  1. Combine half-and-half, egg whites, confectioners' sugar, brown sugar, vanilla, lemon extract, and salt in a bowl; whip together with a whisk or electric whisk until well blended, about 30 seconds. Pour mixture into a large, flat container
  2. Put one bread slice in the container and flip so it is coated on both sides, almost saturated.
  3. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a frying pan over medium heat until sizzling. Add soaked bread slice to the pan; you can do more than one depending on the size of the pan. Fry until medium brown and edges are crispy, about 1 1/2 minutes per side. Continue cooking in batches, dipping bread and frying, and adding butter
  4. Serve French toast with maple syrup and add whipped cream on top
